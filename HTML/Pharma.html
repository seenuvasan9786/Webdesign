01.MUCINE

  • It is a group event.
  • A group contain two members.
  • The music will play, one of the team members should wear the headphone and he/she should predict the word (tablet or pharma related).

02.LUCKY TAB

  • It is a individual game.
  • The chairs will be arranged in a circle manner. And at the bottom of chair there will be a name of the tablet.
  • Music will play. When the music stops the participants should occupy in the chair.
  • we announce one tablet and the participant in that chair should eliminated.
  • Finally the one who bear his/her lucky tablet will win.
