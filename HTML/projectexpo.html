Project Expo

01.Rules and Regulation

  • ID card is necessary for all the participants.
  • Maximum of 4 students per project will be allowed.
  • Registration fee per head is 300Rs (including Food).
  • Students need to specify the stream of their project before the Expo.
  • The model should be in a good working condition and the students must bring their necessary accessories. General purpose equipment such as power supply will be provided.
  • At the time of presentation,students are requested to submit a hard copy of the abstract.
  • The prizes will be awarded based on the presentation, viva and model presentation.
