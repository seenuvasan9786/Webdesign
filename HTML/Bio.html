01.FIR

  • A Video Clip or Image will be Shared on the Screen
  • Participants should observe the Image/Video Clearly.
  • Time duration of 30 Minutes will be given to think about the Shared image/video.
  • Finally, the participants will be asked to narrate their unique identification about the image/video at best as they can.

02.Interlingual Warble

  • Subtitle (in English) of a Song will be shared on the screen.
  • Time duration of 30 seconds will be given for the participants to guess the song using the subtitle.

03.Freekie Talkie

  • Physical Balance will be given (Eg; lemon and spoon, water bottle, scale etc.,).
  • Questions will be asked to the participants (or activity).
  • The participant have to respond to the question/activity with physical balance.
