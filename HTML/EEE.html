Electrical and Electronics Engineering

01.Circuitrix

  • Total Number of rounds is 2 (1 prelim + 1 final).
  • In Round 1-30 multiple choice questions will be given.
  • For Round- 2, Five team will be selected and each team have 2 members
  • In Round 2- Each team has to debug two circuits
  • Participants have to correct the collapsed circuit within the stipulated time.
  • The one who corrects the collapsed circuits ahead of others is declared as winner.

02.Mono act

  • Participants will be allotted 5 minutes to prepare for the act
  • The Time limit is 1-3 minutes to perform their act.
  • The Thechial teams would be given on the spot
  • Obscenity and offensive gestures are strictly not allowed
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding

03.Technical Quiz
